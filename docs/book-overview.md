# Supply Chain Core Concepts for the Grocery Business

## Target Audience

This book is designed for Quality Engineers and IT professionals who are new to grocery retail supply chain domain concepts.

It is helpful for people who:

- Support testing, DevOps, product, or integration teams
- Need clarity on end-to-end retail workflows
- Want to understand who does what across supply chain operations
- Need to map business concepts to quality engineering scenarios

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this book, you will:

- Understand the end-to-end grocery supply chain lifecycle
- Learn key business processes and handoffs
- Identify business roles and responsibilities
- Gain exposure to major systems and software platforms
- Connect business concepts with QE test design and validation

## Chapter Pattern Used Throughout

Each chapter follows the same format:

1. Concept Explanation
1. Business Use Case
1. Users Involved
1. Tools and Technologies Used

## Recommended Reading Path

1. Foundations
1. Planning and Procurement
1. Operations
1. Systems and QE Mapping
1. Lifecycle Walkthrough and Glossary
