## Concept Explanation

Replenishment ensures the right quantity reaches stores at the right time. Store operations convert inventory into shelf availability. Fulfillment handles customer order execution for online and omnichannel journeys.

## Business Use Case

Weekend promotions increase demand for snacks and beverages. Replenishment rules trigger extra store shipments. At the same time, online click-and-collect orders consume shelf stock, so systems must keep inventory accurate across channels.

## Users Involved

- Replenishment Analyst: tunes min/max and safety stock rules
- Store Inventory Lead: monitors stock and shelf gaps
- E-commerce Fulfillment Team: picks online orders
- Customer Service Team: handles substitutions and order issues

## Tools and Technologies Used

- Store replenishment and allocation systems
- POS and inventory visibility services
- Order Management System (OMS)
- Handheld store picking and fulfillment apps
