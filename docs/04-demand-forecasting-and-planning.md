## Concept Explanation

Demand forecasting estimates future sales using historical sales, seasonality, promotions, local events, and weather patterns. Planning then converts demand into inventory and ordering targets.

## Business Use Case

For Thanksgiving week, turkey, baking goods, and dairy demand rises sharply. Forecast models are adjusted, safety stock is increased, and suppliers receive early purchase commitments.

## Users Involved

- Demand Planner: creates baseline and adjusted forecasts
- Category Manager: adds business context (promotions, pricing)
- Supplier Account Manager: confirms capacity
- QE Engineer: verifies forecast integration and exception handling

## Tools and Technologies Used

- Forecasting engines and planning modules
- Data science or machine learning models
- Promotion planning tools
- KPI dashboards (MAPE, bias, fill rate)
