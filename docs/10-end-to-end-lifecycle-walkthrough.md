## Concept Explanation

End-to-end lifecycle means tracking one product from planning to customer purchase and feedback loop. This helps teams understand dependencies and points where quality issues can surface.

## Business Use Case

Use one SKU (for example, yogurt cup) and follow the full chain:

1. Forecast demand by region and week
1. Raise supplier purchase order
1. Receive and store product in temperature-controlled DC zone
1. Dispatch to stores based on replenishment policy
1. Sell via store POS and online fulfillment
1. Capture wastage/returns and feed insights back to planning

## Users Involved

- Planner, Buyer, Supplier, DC Team, Store Team, E-commerce Team, Finance, and QE

## Tools and Technologies Used

- Forecasting and planning systems
- ERP procurement and finance modules
- WMS and TMS for movement and tracking
- POS/OMS for sales and fulfillment
- BI dashboards for KPI review and optimization

## Typical KPIs Across the Lifecycle

- Forecast Accuracy
- Supplier Fill Rate
- On-Time In-Full Delivery (OTIF)
- Shelf Availability
- Inventory Turnover
- Wastage Percentage
