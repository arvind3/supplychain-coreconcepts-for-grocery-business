## Concept Explanation

Supply chain outcomes depend on coordinated decisions across business and IT roles. Clear ownership reduces delays, rework, and production incidents.

## Business Use Case

When supplier lead time increases unexpectedly, planners adjust forecast assumptions, buyers revise order dates, transportation teams update carrier booking, and QA validates updated business rules in staging before production rollout.

## Users Involved

- Planner: demand and inventory planning
- Buyer: supplier order execution
- Supplier: product supply and confirmations
- Warehouse Supervisor: inbound and outbound flow control
- Store Operations Lead: in-store inventory and execution
- QE/QA Engineer: process validation and test coverage
- Product Owner: prioritization and process alignment

## Tools and Technologies Used

- Collaboration tools (ticketing, incident, and workflow platforms)
- Retail planning and procurement suites
- Test management and automation platforms
- Business intelligence tools for KPI monitoring
