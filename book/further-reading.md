# Further Reading

## Books and Long-Form Material

- Grocery retail operations and category management handbooks.
- Supply chain analytics and forecasting methodology texts.
- Warehouse operations and cold-chain execution guides.

## Suggested Internal Study Sequence

1. Review one high-velocity category end to end.
1. Compare forecast assumptions vs store-level sell-through.
1. Trace supplier service misses to downstream shelf outcomes.
1. Analyze one month of fulfillment substitutions for root causes.

## Practical Exercises

- Build a weekly exception review using top revenue SKUs.
- Create a store-cluster demand sensitivity matrix.
- Design a risk-based QE scenario suite for promotion weeks.
